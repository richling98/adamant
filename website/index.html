How it works

01
Record

Open Zoom, Meet, or Teams. Hit record. Adamant captures mic + system audio locally.

02
Transcribe

Whisper runs on your Mac's chips. Every word appears live — no internet, no round trips.

03
Summarize

Decisions, action items, key points — auto-generated by your LLM of choice.

Features

Local transcription

Whisper.cpp runs on-device. No audio ever leaves your Mac.

AI summaries

Ollama, Claude, Groq, or OpenRouter. Your LLM, your choice.

Private by default

No cloud, no accounts, no telemetry. Stored in SQLite on your machine.

Folders & search

Organize meetings into folders. Full-text search across all transcripts.

Apple Silicon

Optimized for M-series chips with Metal GPU acceleration for Whisper.

Open source

MIT license. Read the code. Self-hostable. No vendor lock-in.
