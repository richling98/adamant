How it works

From meeting to summary
in seconds — on your device.

No accounts. No setup. No waiting for the cloud.

1

Join your meeting

Open Zoom, Meet, or Teams as you normally would. Hit record in Adamant. That's it — Adamant captures both your mic and system audio locally.

2

Real-time transcription

Whisper AI runs on your Mac's chips — no internet required. Every word is transcribed live, right in the app, with zero latency to a remote server.

3

AI summary & Q&A

When you're done, Adamant generates a full summary — decisions, action items, key points. Ask Adam anything about your meetings, past or present.

Features

Everything you need.
Nothing you don't.

100% Private

Your meeting data never leaves your machine. No cloud uploads, no third-party processing, no surveillance.

Real-Time Transcription

Powered by Whisper.cpp on Apple Silicon. Words appear as they're spoken — no waiting, no round trips.

AI Summaries

Choose your AI: Ollama (local), Claude, Groq, or OpenRouter. Get decisions, action items, and key points automatically.

Ask Adam

Chat with your entire meeting history. "What did we decide about pricing last week?" Adam knows — and he never forgets.

Instant Search

Full-text search across every transcript, note, and summary. Partial words work too — type "quart" to find "quarterly".

Folders & Organization

Organize meetings into nested folders. Keep Q3 planning separate from 1:1s. Find anything instantly — no scrolling through a sea of notes.

Our Belief
"Your conversations are yours.
Not ours. Not the cloud's."

Every AI meeting tool wants to store your conversations on their servers. Adamant doesn't. We built a tool that is genuinely, architecturally incapable of reading your meetings — because none of it ever reaches us.

No cloud sync
Meetings stored locally in SQLite on your Mac. Your files, your format.
No microphone data transmitted
Audio is processed by Whisper on your device and immediately discarded. Never uploaded.
Works fully offline
With a local model (Ollama), Adamant works with zero internet connection. Always.
FAQ

Common questions

Yes, fully. Transcription uses Whisper.cpp which runs locally on your Mac's Neural Engine. If you use Ollama for AI summaries, the entire app works with no internet connection at all. Cloud AI providers (Claude, Groq) require internet, but transcription never does.

For transcription: Whisper (tiny through large-v3-turbo) via whisper.cpp. For AI summaries and Ask Adam: Ollama (local), Claude (Anthropic), Groq, or any OpenRouter-compatible model. You pick what fits your workflow and privacy requirements.

Yes. Adamant is free and open source under the MIT license. You can download it, use it, modify it, and distribute it freely. There is no paid plan, no freemium gate, no subscription.

Yes. Adamant captures system audio directly from macOS — it's not a bot that joins your call. It captures whatever is playing through your speakers (or virtual audio device), so it works with any meeting platform: Zoom, Google Meet, Microsoft Teams, Slack huddles, or anything else.

Apple Silicon (M1 or later) running macOS 13 or above. The app is optimised for Metal GPU acceleration and Apple Neural Engine — this is what makes local Whisper transcription fast and efficient. Intel Macs are not currently supported.
